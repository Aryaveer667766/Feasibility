Sales Playbook

Standard operating procedures, scripts, and frameworks.

Discovery Call Framework

1. The Hook (First 30s): "Hi [Name], I noticed [Specific compliment about their content/business]. I have an idea that could add [X result]. Do you have 2 minutes?"

2. The Pain Identification: "What is the biggest bottleneck preventing you from scaling your content output right now?"

3. The Pitch: "At Feasibility Media, we specialize in building visual retention engines. We solve [Their Pain] by [Our Solution]."

4. The Close: "Does it make sense to schedule a 15-minute deep dive on Tuesday to show you the exact strategy?"

Objection Handling

"We don't have budget": "I completely understand. If budget wasn't an issue, is this a solution you feel would actually grow your business?" (If yes, negotiate scope. If no, establish value proposition first).

"We already have an agency": "That’s great. We aren't looking to replace them today. We are a specialized agency focusing purely on [Specific Niche]. Can we do a test run on your next campaign to see how we compare?"

"Just send me an email": "I absolutely will. But just so I don't send you generic info, what is your #1 priority right now?" (Keep them engaged).

Cold Email Template

Subject: Quick idea for [Company Name]

Hi [Name],

I was checking out your recent campaign and loved [Specific Detail]. However, I noticed you might be leaving engagement on the table with your current video pacing.

We are a specialized media agency that helps brands like yours increase retention by 40% through high-end editing.

I actually put together a quick 2-minute loom video showing exactly how we would upgrade your next release. Worth a watch?

Best,

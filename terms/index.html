Operational Protocol \& Legal Framework

This Master Service Agreement (the "Agreement") governs the relationship between Feasibility Media Group ("Agency") and You ("Client"). By authorizing this document, you agree to be bound by the following infrastructure terms.

1. Social Media Management & Access

  • Authorization & Access: The Client grants the Agency the authority to access, manage, and publish content on the Client's designated social media accounts for the duration of this Agreement. The Client agrees to provide necessary login credentials or "Partner/Admin" access via platform business managers.
  • Platform Compliance & Liability (Anti-Ban Clause): The Agency strictly adheres to the terms of service of all major platforms. However, the Client acknowledges that social media platforms frequently update their algorithms and policies. The Agency shall not be liable for any account suspension, restriction, shadow-ban, or permanent ban imposed by the social media platforms, provided the Agency has acted in good faith. The Agency is not responsible for rectifying account restrictions caused by the Client's previous activities or third-party bot services.
  • Community Management: If included in the Client's tier, the Agency will reply to standard comments and messages during business hours. The Agency is not responsible for complex customer support or crisis communication.

2. Client Obligations

  • Content Materials: The Client agrees to provide all necessary raw materials (high-res logos, brand guidelines, footage) required for the Agency to perform the Services within 48 hours of onboarding.
  • Approval Process: The Client must provide feedback on drafts within 48 hours of delivery. If no feedback is received, the content is deemed "Approved" to maintain the posting schedule.
  • Legal Validity: The Client guarantees that all claims, copyrights, and product information provided to the Agency for publication are legally valid and owned by the Client.

3. Fees, Payment & Taxes

  • Invoicing: Services are billed in advance to secure infrastructure bandwidth. Invoices are generated on the 1st of every month. Payment is strictly due within 5 days of invoice receipt via NEFT, UPI, or Bank Transfer.
  • Late Payment: Invoices not paid within 7 days of the due date will incur a late fee of 2% per month. The Agency reserves the right to suspend all production if payment is overdue by more than 10 days.
  • Third-Party Costs & Ad Spend: The Agency retainer fee does not cover your advertising spend. The Client is solely responsible for funding their own ad accounts (Meta, Google, etc.) directly via their own credit cards.

4. Intellectual Property (IP) Rights

  • Transfer of Ownership: Upon full payment of the monthly invoice, the Agency assigns to the Client 100% ownership rights to the final, exported Deliverables.
  • Agency Rights: The Agency retains ownership of internal tools, proprietary templates, and working files (e.g., Premiere Pro project files).
  • Portfolio Rights: The Client grants the Agency a non-exclusive right to display the final work and generalized performance metrics (e.g., Views, ROAS percentages) in its portfolio for self-promotion, unless a strict Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) is executed.

5. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to treat all non-public information (logins, trade secrets, financial data, unreleased strategies) as strictly confidential. This obligation survives the termination of this Agreement for a period of two (2) years.

6. Limitation of Liability & Indemnity

  • Limitation: We engineer content for maximum algorithmic reach. However, algorithms and market conditions are out of our control. Feasibility Media does not legally guarantee specific financial returns or subscriber counts. The Agency's total liability shall not exceed the total fees paid by the Client in the three (3) months preceding the claim. No liability is assumed for indirect or consequential damages.
  • Indemnity: The Client indemnifies the Agency against legal claims arising from content provided directly by the Client or false claims regarding Client products/services.

7. Non-Solicitation

During the term of this Agreement and for 12 months thereafter, the Client agrees not to solicit, poach, or hire any Agency employee or contractor. A breach of this clause incurs a placement fee equal to 50% of the employee's annual salary.

8. Term and Termination

  • No Lock-ins: This Agreement operates on a rolling month-to-month basis.
  • Termination: Either party may terminate with 30 days' written notice.
  • Effect: The Client is responsible for paying for all work up to the termination date. The Agency will transfer all finalized assets upon clearing of final dues.

9. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of India. Any disputes arising from this Agreement shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts located in Ranchi, Jharkhand, India.
